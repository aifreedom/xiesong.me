About Me

A senior college student grounded solidly in the basis of computer science at Huazhong University of Science and Technology(HUST) in Wuhan, China, I love Computer Science, Algorithms, Artificial Intelligence, Mathematics, Logic, Physics, and Critical Thinking.

My participation in various academic activities in high school and college stimulated my interests in algorithms and mathematics. I won the first prize in National Olympiad in Informatics in Province(NOIP) in high school, which qualified me to be admitted by HUST exempted from the National Higher University Entrance Examination. And I won a Bronze Medal in ACM-International Collegiate Programming Contest(ACM-ICPC) and a Honorable Mention in Mathematical Contest in Modeling(MCM). And I am the leader of my team in both of the contests.

Linux was my major operating system, until I switched to Mac OS X recently. I am impressed by its Philosophy of Freedom. And my notes about Linux can be found on my blog.

Emacs is my favourable text editor, and I write Emacs Lisp. And I also organized my experience about Emacs on my blog.

While C/C++ is my "mother tongue", Python is now my daily language. I have initiated several projects in Python:

  • PyIagno – a Reversi Game, my first project on github;
  • A system monitor for Linux using pygtkmvc as the GUI framework;
  • A Web Service deployed on Google App Engine for BookAddict – an Windows Phone 7 app providing book information by scanning barcode.

I use LaTeX to typeset my papers and documents. My CV is typeset by LaTeX with the help of moderncv. You can check out my articles (in Chinese) on how to configure LaTeX and introductions to several widely used LaTeX macro packages on my blog.

After three years of studying computer science, I realized that it is Artificial Intelligence that attracts me more than any other field in computer science, because I have long admired those who make intelligent machines. So, I am now applying for graduate stydies in Artificial Intelligence.
